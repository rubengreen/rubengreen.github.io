"Rubie vibrates during a set; bellowing with the might of an army and softly plunging like a cleavage - very soon you’re immersed in a total feeling" - TheProphet4Profit

Rubie exorcises maleness in the eclectic and experimental, through the body of a sad spotlit songstress. They channel their intricate songwriting craft through a performance art lens, often stretching the limits of minimal vocal-centered live setups. Sailing on a soup, weathering binary storms, learning to captain.

FFO: Arthur Russell, PJ Harvey, Julia Holter, Anohni
Current live lineup: Rubie Green on vocals and various instruments. Kit Denison on percussion.
