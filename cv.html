Exhibitions, Performances and Awards

June 2017 - Completed a month long residency with Sophie Mak-Schram in Aljustrel, Portugal, invited by Atalaia Artes Performativas. Continuing our collaborative performance-tour project Tail Bend Travel, we developed The Maybe Museum – An hour long walking tour of Aljustrel, blending local history with our own fictional stories and poetry.
22nd June 2017 - Performed an experimental acapella-vocal-processing set at Pornceptual does about:blank, Berlin.
9th March 2017 - Closed the show at Metaphonica III, a music/art intersection concert at Central St. Martins, London. Headlining alongside Penny Rimbaud, ACM and Charismatic Megafauna.
14th January 2017 - Exhibited Between Two Islands at 14th Cinema, Shoreditch, London, curated by Divine Southgate Smith, Charlotte Taylor and Bence Magyarlaki.
1st October 2016 - Performed The Jagged Rocks Below, a theatrical mythical poetry tour of Canary Wharf, with Sophie Mak-Schram. Part of Houserules’ Guided Tours, for Art Licks Weekend.
September 2016 - One of six artists participating in the Athens U-KAVE month long residency in Keramikos, Athens. Exhibited A Cocoon, and performed A Cocoon/A Space to Call your Own and Be Your Daughter Plait My Hair at the open studio on the 20th Sept.
July 2016 - Performed in support of ROCH on her South East London Residency
June 2016 - Exhibited Two Artists in Conversation in collaboration with Adelaida Antúnez Egurbide in the Tate Modern turbine hall, as part of the new Tate Modern’s opening weekend.
February 2016 - Exhibited/Performed Organ Dance at CHURCH, Group show @ Asylum London
January 2016 - Performed at Rhythm Machine @ Summerhall Edinburgh, in a part-improvised music and spoken word collaboration with Serafima Mehovich
November 2015 - Exhibited Great Green Tour and volunteered at Remember Nature, a collaborative exhibition between Central Saint Martins and Gustav Metzger
May 2015 - Winner of Books for the 21st Century, a competition run between Central Saint Martins and Swiss bookbinder Bubu. Won a trip to Switzerland to visit Bubu’s factory.
March 2015 - Exhibited I Don’t Know How to Play the German Concert Zither at STRANGELOVE Moving Image Festival, directed by David Gryn and Terry Smith

Other Experience

29th August 2016 - Released WALK OUT EP on Bandcamp, Spotify & iTunes
July 2016 - Participation in HOUSERULES summer school for the whole month, engaging in a discussion around alternative art education as well as producing lots of work.
June 2016 - Organised Beck Road and the Artist Community – pilgrimage/discussion; a public event where we walked from our shared studio in Archway to Beck Road, the location of a squatting artist community in the 80s. En route discussing our anxieties relating to studio space, as emerging artists in London.
18th October 2015 - Independently released debut album Fully Formed
October 2015 - Collaborated with Beatrice Vermeir & Ben Schmechel of SOUP collective, organising their HOUSERULES Guided Tour of Peckham. There were lots vegetables.
