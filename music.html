"Rubie vibrates during a set; bellowing with the might of an army and softly plunging like a cleavage - very soon you’re immersed in a total feeling" - TheProphet4Profit

Rubie is a binary cleaver, both a sad spotlit songstress and a de-male-ing of the out there, ambient and jazz-inflected. They are sailing across soup through gender storms, learning to captain.

current live lineup: Rubie on vocals, guitar, piano. Kit Denison on percussion.
