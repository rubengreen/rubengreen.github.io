Established in 2016 at the curve of the mid-millennial turn, Tail Bend Travel offers the casually curious traveller a walking experience like no other. Twisting together everything you might like to know about a place and more, Tail Bend will transform the way you view every turning.

We offer guided tours in person, audio and video format, and many of our tours include complimentary tasters of local and historically relevant delicacies.
